<p><strong>The Anti-lock Brake Systems for Cars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-channel ABS</li><li>Two-channel ABS</li><li>Three-channel ABS</li><li>Four-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-lock Brake Systems (ABS) enhance vehicle safety by preventing wheel lock-up during braking. One-channel ABS controls a single rear wheel, primarily for older models. Two-channel ABS manages front brakes separately from rear ones, improving stability. Three-channel ABS adds control for one rear wheel alongside the two front wheels, offering better performance. Four-channel ABS optimizes braking for each wheel individually, providing maximum control and safety, making it the most advanced option for modern vehicles and enhancing overall driving dynamics.</p></p>
