<p><p>The genotyping market encompasses various technologies for analyzing genetic variations. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies DNA for detailed study. Sequencing provides precise nucleotide order, enhancing understanding of genetic sequences. Microarrays enable simultaneous analysis of multiple genes, facilitating high-throughput genotyping. Electrophoresis separates DNA fragments by size for comparison. MALDI-TOF (Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption/Ionization Time-of-Flight) utilizes mass spectrometry for rapid identification of genetic variants. Together, these methods support advancements in personalized medicine and genetic research.</p></p>
