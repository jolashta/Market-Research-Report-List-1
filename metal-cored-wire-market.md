<p><p>The metal cored wire market includes several types tailored for specific metallurgical needs. Calcium silicide cored wire (CaSi) enhances steel's deoxidation and desulfurization, while calcium solid cored wire (Ca) serves as a powerful deoxidizer. Pure carbon cored wire (C) is used for carbon adjustment in steel, whereas ferro calcium cored wire (CaFe) provides both calcium and iron benefits. Other variants may include specialty alloys designed for unique applications in steelmaking and casting processes, improving quality and performance.</p></p>
