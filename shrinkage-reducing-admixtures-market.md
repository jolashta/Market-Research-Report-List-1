<p><p>Shrinkage-Reducing Admixtures (SRAs) are specialized chemical additives used in concrete and cement-based products to minimize shrinkage during the curing and drying processes. These admixtures work by altering the surface tension of the water in the mix, reducing the amount of water absorbed by the surrounding air and thus mitigating the potential for cracking. This property makes SRAs increasingly popular in various construction applications, particularly in high-performance concrete, roads, and pavements.</p><p>The Shrinkage-Reducing Admixtures Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period, driven by an escalating demand for durable construction materials and a rising focus on sustainable building practices.
