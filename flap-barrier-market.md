<p><ul><li>Single Movement</li><li>Double Movement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flap barrier markets are classified into two types: single movement and double movement. </p><p>In a single movement market, the flap barrier operates in one direction, allowing entry or exit but not both simultaneously, enhancing security and controlling access. </p><p>Conversely, a double movement market features flaps that move in two directions, accommodating bidirectional access. This type is ideal for high-traffic areas, allowing more fluid entry and exit, thereby improving efficiency while still maintaining control over access points.</p></p>
