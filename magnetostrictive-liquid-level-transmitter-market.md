<p><p>The magnetostrictive liquid level transmitter market includes three main types: Straight Rod Type Transmitters, Flange Type Transmitters, and Screw-Type Transmitters. Straight Rod Type Transmitters are characterized by their vertical alignment, suitable for deep tanks. Flange Type Transmitters are mounted on tank flanges, offering secure connections and easy maintenance. Screw-Type Transmitters utilize threaded connections, ideal for compact applications and where space is limited. Each type caters to specific installation needs and environments, enhancing accuracy in liquid level measurement.</p></p>
