<p><p>The green roofing market is categorized into extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive systems. Extensive green roofing features lightweight vegetation, requiring minimal maintenance and limited substrate depth, ideal for flat roofs. Semi-intensive roofs combine deeper substrates, supporting a wider variety of plants, leading to moderate maintenance needs. Intensive green roofing is characterized by deep soil layers, allowing for diverse and lush landscapes, resembling traditional gardens, but necessitating significant maintenance and structural support. Each type serves different aesthetic, environmental, and functional needs.</p></p>
