<p><ul><li>Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy</li><li>Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz spectroscopy system market comprises two main types: Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy (THz-TDS) and Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy (THz-FDS). THz-TDS utilizes pulsed terahertz radiation to capture time-resolved information, offering insights into material properties and dynamics. In contrast, THz-FDS employs continuous wave terahertz signals to analyze frequency-dependent absorption and reflection characteristics. Both techniques enable detailed material characterization across various applications, including pharmaceuticals, biomedicine, and material science, driving growth in sectors that require precise material analysis.</p></p>
