<p><strong>The Plasma Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure PRP</li><li>Leucocyte-rich PRP</li><li>Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plasma therapy market encompasses various types based on the composition and use of platelet-rich plasma (PRP) components. Pure PRP, characterized by a high concentration of platelets, is primarily used for tissue healing and rejuvenation. Leucocyte-rich PRP contains a substantial number of white blood cells, enhancing its efficacy in combating inflammation and promoting healing. Leukocyte-rich fibrin (L-PRF) features a three-dimensional fibrin matrix and is used in regenerative practices, supporting cell migration and tissue regeneration more effectively than other types.</p></p>
