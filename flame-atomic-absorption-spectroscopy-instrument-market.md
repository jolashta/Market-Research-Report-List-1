<p><p>Flame Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy (FAAS) is a powerful analytical technique used to determine the concentration of elements in various samples. The FAAS instrument consists of a light source, nebulizer, flame, and detector, making it vital across diverse sectors such as environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and metallurgy.</p><p>The Flame Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy Instrument Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for accurate and rapid analysis in laboratories.
