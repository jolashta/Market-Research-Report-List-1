<p><p>The hot runner temperature controllers market is segmented into two main types: modular and non-modular. Modular systems consist of individual temperature control units that can be easily added or removed, allowing for scalability and flexibility in managing multiple heating zones. In contrast, non-modular systems feature a fixed configuration, typically with fewer customizable options, which may be simpler but less adaptable. Both types are essential for optimizing the temperature management of hot runner systems in injection molding processes, enhancing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
