<p><p>The oil burner nozzles market is primarily categorized into solid and hollow types. Solid nozzles deliver a continuous stream of fuel, optimizing combustion efficiency and minimizing emissions. They are typically used in applications requiring precise fuel atomization. In contrast, hollow nozzles produce a spray pattern, allowing for better mixing with air and achieving efficient combustion in larger systems. Both types cater to various industrial and residential heating needs, enhancing energy efficiency and performance in oil-burning equipment.</p></p>
