The PHA market is categorized into several types: PHB (Polyhydroxybutyrate), known for its strong mechanical properties; PHBV (Polyhydroxybutyrate-co-valerate), offering enhanced flexibility; PHBHx (Polyhydroxybutyrate-co-hexanoate), known for its elastomeric qualities; PHB4B (Polyhydroxybutyrate-co-4-butyrolactone), with improved thermal stability; and Other types, including various copolymers with tailored properties. These variations cater to diverse applications in packaging, agriculture, and medical fields.</p></p>
