<p><p>The Arthroscopy Radio Frequency System market is categorized into two main types: Bipolar and Single Pole. Bipolar systems utilize two electrodes for energy delivery, allowing precise targeting of tissue, minimizing damage to surrounding areas, and enhancing safety during procedures. In contrast, Single Pole systems use one active electrode and rely on grounding pads, which can lead to broader tissue effects. Both types serve vital roles in arthroscopic procedures, offering distinct benefits depending on the surgical requirements and desired outcomes.</p></p>
