<p><strong>The Universal Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Needle Bearing Universal Joints</li><li>Plain Bearing Universal Joints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal Couplings market comprises primarily two types: Needle Bearing Universal Joints and Plain Bearing Universal Joints. Needle Bearing Universal Joints utilize needle bearings to enhance load capacity and reduce friction, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Conversely, Plain Bearing Universal Joints feature simpler construction with minimal friction, ideal for low-speed, low-load scenarios. Both types are essential in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and machinery, ensuring efficient torque transmission and flexibility in movement across shafts.</p></p>
