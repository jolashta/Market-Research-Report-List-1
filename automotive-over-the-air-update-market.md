<p><strong>The Automotive Over-The-Air Update Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Firmware Over-The-Air Technology (FOTA)</li><li>Software Over-The-Air Technology (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Over-The-Air Update market encompasses two primary types: Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) and Software Over-The-Air (SOTA). FOTA technology enables the remote updating of a vehicle's firmware, ensuring critical system components, like engine control units, operate optimally. In contrast, SOTA focuses on enhancing software applications, allowing for improvements in user interfaces, navigation systems, and infotainment features. Both technologies enhance vehicle performance, security, and user experience while reducing the need for physical service center visits.</p></p>
