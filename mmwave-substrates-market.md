<p><p>The mmWave substrates market comprises several key types, including LCP, PI, PTFE, and others. LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) substrates offer low loss and high thermal stability, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. PI (Polyimide) substrates are known for their excellent thermal and mechanical properties, suitable for flexible electronics. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) substrates are valued for their low dielectric constant and high-frequency performance. Other substrates encompass a range of materials that offer specific advantages for various mmWave applications, enhancing connectivity and performance.</p></p>
