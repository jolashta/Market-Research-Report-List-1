NCM (Nickel-Cobalt-Manganese) and NCA (Nickel-Cobalt-Aluminum) cells are known for their high energy density and performance, making them ideal for long-range electric vehicles. Other technologies, such as lithium-titanate and solid-state batteries, are emerging to provide specific advantages like faster charging and improved lifespan.</p></p>
