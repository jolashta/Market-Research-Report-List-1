<p><strong>The Fertilizer Nitrogen Stabilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Urease Inhibitor</li><li>Nitrification Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fertilizer nitrogen stabilizers enhance nitrogen efficiency in agricultural applications by reducing nutrient loss. Urease inhibitors prevent the enzyme urease from converting urea into ammonia, thereby minimizing volatilization and improving nitrogen availability to crops. Nitrification inhibitors, on the other hand, slow down the conversion of ammonium to nitrate, reducing leaching and denitrification losses in the soil. Together, these stabilizers contribute to more sustainable farming practices, promoting better crop yields while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
