<p><p>The Beam Viewing Card market consists of various types based on the wavelength of light they utilize: Infrared (IR), Near-Infrared (NIR), Ultraviolet (UV), and Visible (VIS). IR cards are designed for thermal imaging, detecting heat signatures. NIR cards enhance imaging in low light conditions. UV cards are used for fluorescence and detecting specific materials, while VIS cards focus on the visible spectrum for general imaging applications. Each type serves distinct sectors, such as security, scientific research, and industrial inspections.</p></p>
