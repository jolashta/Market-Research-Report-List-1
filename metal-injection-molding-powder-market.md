<p><p>Metal Injection Molding Powder (MIM powder) is a composite material used in the metal injection molding process, which combines the benefits of powder metallurgy and plastic injection molding. This technique allows for the production of complex, high-precision metal parts with intricate geometries that can be difficult to achieve through traditional manufacturing methods. MIM powders are typically composed of a metal powdered alloy mixed with a binder, which is then shaped and sintered to create durable components widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and medical devices.</p><p>The Metal Injection Molding Powder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for lightweight and high-strength components across various sectors.
