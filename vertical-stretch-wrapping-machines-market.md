<p><p>Vertical stretch wrapping machines are categorized into semi-automatic and automatic types. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention for loading and securing loads before wrapping, making them ideal for medium-volume operations that need flexibility. In contrast, automatic machines streamline the entire process, featuring conveyors and integrated systems that enhance efficiency for high-volume production environments. Both types cater to different business needs, balancing cost, speed, and operational demands to effectively secure and protect products for storage or shipment.</p></p>
